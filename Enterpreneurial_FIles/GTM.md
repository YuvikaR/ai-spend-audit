# GTM Strategy: The Credex "Efficiency Framework

## 1. The Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**The "Chaotic Scale" Persona**

* **Target:** The person in charge of operations the finance lead or the CTO.

* **Company Profile:** Startups with 30 to 250 employees that have gotten money from investors.

* **The Technical DNA:** These companies are really into engineering work from home and use intelligence.

* **The Spending Threshold:** They spend between $5,000 and $50,000 per month on AI tools.

### **The Psychological State: "Financial Fog"**

The person is not looking for AI tools. They are feeling bad about money.

* **The Chaos:** The engineering team uses one tool marketing uses another and the leaders are trying out something

* **The Trigger:** The person in charge of money asks, "Why did our spending on software go up 400% since last year?"

---

## 2. Intent "Trigger" Moments

We talk to the person when they are already wondering if they are doing things right.

| Intent Category | Search or Social Queries |

| --- | ---

| **Budgeting** | "How to save money on AI tools" |

| **Renewal Panic** | "What is the AI tool for a startup" |

| **Tool Overlap** | "Which AI tool is better for development teams" |

| **Crisis Auditing** | "How to cut spending on software" |

---

## 3. Digital. Gravity Centers

Where people talk about their problems with software.

* **Online Forums:** Places like Reddit where people discuss software and money.

* **Private Groups:** Places like Slack or Discord where people talk about money and software.

* **Twitter:** Groups of people who talk about working

* **LinkedIn:** People who work at startups and talk about having a system.

---

## 4. The 30-Day "Zero-Dollar" Sprint

**Goal:** Get 100 people to try our audit.

### **Phase A: The "AI Stack Review"**

Do not post about what our tool can do. Post about how we can help.

* *“A startup with 60 people was spending $12,000 per month on AI tools. We helped them cut that by 30% in 60 seconds.”*

* **Where to post:** LinkedIn and Twitter.

### **Phase B: The "Comparison"**

People want to know if they are behind.

* **How to get them:** "See how you compare to startups in your area."

* **How it works:** We collect data. Give them a score.

### **Phase C: Manual Outreach**

Send messages to people who are growing their startups.

* **What to say:** "I can help you see where you are wasting money on AI tools. I will send you a report if you want."

* **How to send:** Send them a message.

---

## 5. The "Unfair" Distribution Advantage

**The Report as a Gift**

Most tools are tools. Credex is a way to talk to people.

1. **The Report:** The audit makes a report that leaders can read.

2. **Internal Sharing:** The person who does operations will share the report with the person in charge of money.

3. **Building Trust:** We will make a report using data from companies and this will make Credex the standard for AI tools.

---

We care more about people sharing our report than looking at our website.

| Metric | Target | Significance |

| --- | --- | ---

| **People Visiting the Website** 1,000+ | People know our brand |

| **Completed Audits** | 50+ | Our product is working |

If they feel safe they will use our tool because it will help them with their money problems. Credex is, about helping people with their money problems by using triggers to make them take action. We will use the Credex brand to make our GTM plan work.
