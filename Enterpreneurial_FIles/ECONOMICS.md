## Economics

This analysis looks at how much a customer is worth to us over their lifetime and how much we can spend to get them.

We use a formula to figure this out.

Here are the numbers we use:

* Annual Contract Value: $12,000, which's what mid-market small businesses usually pay

* Gross Margin: 75%, which's the standard for our industry

* Customer Lifetime: 3 Years, which is how long we think customers will stay with us

* Gross Profit LTV: $27,000, which's $36,000 in revenue times 75% margin

2. The Conversion Funnel

We want to know how many people who use our free audit tool will become paying customers.

Here are the steps people take to become customers:

* Audit to Consultation: 12% of people who use the audit tool will talk to us about it

* Consultation to Paid Customer: 20% of people who talk to us will become paying customers

* Blended Audit-to-Close Rate: 2.4% of people who use the audit tool will become paying customers

We think that every 100 audits are worth $28,800 per year which means each audit is worth $288.

3. Channel Strategy and Acquisition Efficiency

We want to know how to get more people to use our audit tool.

Here are the channels we use:

* SEO and Content: we spend $4,000 per month and get 750 audits, which costs $222 per audit

* LinkedIn Organic: we spend $1,500 per month and get 100 audits, which costs $625 per audit

* LinkedIn Paid: we spend $8 per click and get 8 audits per month which costs $4,167 per audit

We think SEO is the way to get audits because we can target people who are looking for things like "AI pricing calculator".

4. Profitability and CAC Payback

We want to make sure we are making money and not spending much to get customers.

Here are our goals:

* Annual Gross Profit per Customer: $9,000

* Maximum Profitable CAC: $3,000

* Required Audit-to-Customer Conversion: 2% of people who use the audit tool must become paying customers

5. Scaling to $1M ARR

We want to make $1 million per year in recurring revenue.

To do this we need:

* 3,500 audits

* 195 audits per month

* 2,440 unique visitors per month which is 8% of people who come to our site

6. Strategic Must-Haves for Viability

To make all of this work we need:

I. High-Fidelity Credibility: our audit tool must be accurate

II. The Moment of Urgency: we must show people how money they are losing so they will take action

III. Low-Friction Viral Loops: we must make it easy for people to share our audit tool with others

IV. Enterprise Upside: we must be able to get deals with bigger companies to make more money

Our status is economically viable but we are worried about people trusting our audit tool and its accuracy.

Our main focus is, on getting more people to use our audit tool through SEO and organic distribution.
